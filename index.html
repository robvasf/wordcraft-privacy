Privacy Policy – Wordcraft: Warden Runes

This privacy policy explains how Wordcraft: Warden Runes handles user data. The game is designed to collect the minimum amount of information required to provide gameplay features such as leaderboards and optional online services.

Information We Collect

Wordcraft: Warden Runes does not collect or store personally identifiable information on its own servers.

The game may use the following services provided by Google:

  • Google Play Games Services – used for optional sign-in, achievements and leaderboards.
  • Google AdMob – used to display advertisements.

These services may collect data such as:

  • Google Play Games player ID
  • Leaderboard scores
  • Game progress related to achievements
  • Device information required to deliver ads

This data is processed and stored by Google according to their privacy policies.

How the Information Is Used

The information collected through Google services is used only to:

  • Display global leaderboards
  • Enable achievements and ranking systems
  • Deliver advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Maintain fair gameplay and competition

Data Storage

Wordcraft: Warden Runes does not store personal user data on independent servers. All online services are handled through Google Play Games Services and Google AdMob.

Data Deletion

Users may remove their game data associated with Google Play Games by managing their Google account settings.

More information about managing and deleting Google account data can be found here:

https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/3024190

Children’s Privacy

Wordcraft: Warden Runes does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Any data processing is handled by Google services according to their policies.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements to the game or changes in platform requirements.
